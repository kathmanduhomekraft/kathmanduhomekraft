Why homemade matters

Made the way it would be made at home.

Seeta Mix Achaar comes out of a home-kitchen tradition, not a factory line. Every jar is cut, spiced and packed by hand in a small batch — which is exactly why it tastes the way it does.

१
Fresh, not stockpiled. We make to order in small batches, so your jar is recently made — never months-old shelf stock.

३

Shipping note

This achaar is made with pressed mustard oil. It's perfect for Nepal and for sending to family inside Nepal — but pressed mustard oil isn't permitted as a food import in the US, EU, Canada, Australia and New Zealand, so we don't ship the jar to those countries directly.
